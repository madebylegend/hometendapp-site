Is HomeTend really for new homeowners?

While we're perfect for new homeowners (especially those within the last 5 years), anyone can benefit from HomeTend. Our AI personalizes everything to your home's specific needs, whether it's brand new or 50 years old.

How does the AI know my home's needs?

Simply tell us your home's age, size, type, and location during setup. Our AI combines this with weather data and maintenance best practices to create your personalized schedule. The more you use the app, the smarter it gets.

Absolutely! HomeTend supports multiple properties, making it perfect for landlords and property managers. Track maintenance across all your homes from one app.
